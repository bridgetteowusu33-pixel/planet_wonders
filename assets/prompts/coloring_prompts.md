# 3D Coloring Page Illustration Prompts

Reference prompts for generating coloring page outline assets with AI image generators.
All images must be **black outlines on transparent background**, high-res (1024x1024+), no fills.

---

## Global Base Prompt (prepend to every generation)

Create a 3D-style illustration for a children's coloring page, designed for ages 5-10.
The illustration should have a soft 3D look with rounded forms, gentle proportions, and a friendly, playful feel.
Style should resemble Pixar-inspired or clay-like characters, but simplified.
Use clear outlines, smooth surfaces, and minimal depth cues.
Avoid realistic textures, harsh shadows, dramatic lighting, or complex shading.
The final output should be suitable for conversion into a 3D coloring page (clear regions, no noise).
White background. Calm, non-scary, non-realistic, storybook quality.

## Important 3D Rules (apply to ALL prompts)

- No photorealism
- No dramatic lighting
- No textures (wood grain, fabric weave, stone)
- No tiny surface details
- No dark shadows

Do use:
- Soft geometry
- Rounded edges
- Simple depth
- Clear regions for coloring
